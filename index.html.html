About This Assessment

Healthcare Organisation (HCO) Categorization Scoring System — Version 1.0

This categorization assessment is designed to provide a structured, objective, and transparent approach for categorising healthcare providers based on their operational capabilities, conduct performance, and geographic context.

It provides an objective, data-driven score out of 100 points based on 18 parameters across three dimensions — Capability, Conduct, and Geography.

The score helps to categorise hospitals consistently, enabling faster and fairer negotiations between payers and providers.
